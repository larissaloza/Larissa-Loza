Water scarcity in Mexico is a worrying problem, with some regions facing severe shortages that barely cover basic needs. Despite the country's abundant water resources, mismanagement and pollution exacerbate the problem. Urgent action is needed to address this challenge through conservation, improved infrastructure and equitable distribution.
This was a web portal created for Conahcyt's national strategic program "Agua", which offers an open access space for data analysis and visualization of information generated under the program.
The movement of water is represented by means of a grid of lines with undulating movements, which in turn represent the graphs and data analysis used to measure this type of problem and possible solutions.
