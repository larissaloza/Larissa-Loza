- MAGMA -

Music Visuals - 2020
Created with Processing, Adobe Photohop, Premiere and After Effects.

I created 3 audio-reactive visuals for Magma Colective, an electronic music band, for the Vortex Experimental film festival, where the show was presented with music and visuals.
Each piece carries the concept of Volcanic Magma, referring to the band's name.
One of them is created with frame-by-frame animation. Another is created in Processing with audio-responsive graphics programming, and the last is a video edit of different clips of erupting volcanoes.
