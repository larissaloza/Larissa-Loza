- MIGRACIÓN -

360° Animation Projection - 2020
Created with Adobe Photoshop and After Effects.

Global migration presents a complex challenge, shaped by a myriad of economic, social, and political factors. As populations continue to grow, millions of individuals are forced to leave their homes in search of safety, opportunity, and a better quality of life.
However, the journey to a new land is often fraught with peril, as migrants face discrimination, exploitation, and the risk of violence along the way. Moreover, host countries grapple with the strain on resources and infrastructure, as well as the complexities of integrating diverse populations into their societies.
Addressing the issue of global migration requires a comprehensive approach that acknowledges the interconnectedness of nations and the shared responsibility to uphold human dignity and rights.
This animation was projected in a 360° room in Museo de las Constituciones (Museum of the Constitutions), Mexico City.
