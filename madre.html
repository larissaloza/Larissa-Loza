- MADRE -

Video Art - 2020
Created with Processing

As an artist committed to the expressive potential of the human form, MADRE is the first work in which I transcend conventional boundaries to unite the experience of corporeality with the immersive power of audiovisual expression.
MADRE transcends the boundaries of traditional art forms. An experimental and conceptual exploration of the figure of the mother, it unfolds the themes of detachment and individual independence, inviting the audience to navigate the contours of memory, identity and human connection.
I personally consider it a synthesis of my artistic and aesthetic expression. It was created with Processing, a dynamic, object-oriented programming language designed for designers and visual artists. Sound, color and shapes respond organically and interactively according to my body movements.
