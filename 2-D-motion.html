- VIVIENDA -

Cover for website project - 2022

The lack of adequate housing in Mexico is a pressing issue, marked by overcrowding, informal settlements, and homelessness. Rapid urbanization and socio-economic disparities exacerbate this problem, leaving millions without safe and affordable housing. Marginalized groups, such as indigenous populations and rural migrants, are disproportionately affected. Comprehensive solutions involving policy reforms, investment in social housing, and community empowerment are essential to address this crisis and ensure dignified living conditions.
This was a web portal created for Conahcyt's national strategic program "Vivienda", which offers an open access space for data analysis and visualization of information generated under the program.

- SOBERANIA ALIMENTARIA -

Cover for website project - 2022

The issue of food sovereignty in Mexico highlights dependence on imported food, agricultural challenges, and unequal access to nutritious meals. Despite agricultural richness, issues such as land concentration and rural poverty persist, threatening food security and cultural heritage. Sustainable agricultural policies and support for smallholder farmers are crucial for promoting local food production and ensuring equitable access to healthy food, fostering resilience and cultural diversity.
This was a web portal created for Conahcyt's national strategic program "Soberania Alimentaria", which offers an open access space for data analysis and visualization of information generated under the program.

- DESAPARECIDOS -

Cover for website project - 2022

The issue of missing persons in Mexico is a grave concern, stemming from violence, organized crime, and systemic failures in law enforcement. Thousands have vanished, leaving families in anguish. Despite efforts, impunity persists, hindering effective investigations. Comprehensive measures, including legal reforms and improved support for victims' families, are urgently needed to address this human rights crisis.
This was a web portal created for Conahcyt's national strategic program "Soberania Alimentaria", which offers an open access space for data analysis and visualization of information generated under the program.
