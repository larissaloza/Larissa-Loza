- CUPCAKES -

3D Art - 2023
Created with Blender

This was one of my first 3D modeling. I created a collection of five cupcakes, each with a different flavor, along with a central cake resting on a wooden base. Inspired by real-life baking, I crafted each item with special attention to details and textures.
Guided by reference images, I began the modeling process, shaping each cupcake and cake to capture their shapes and. Once the foundation was laid, I continued with UV mapping and texturing, adding more realism.
To show the full scope of my creation, I decided to animate a rotating presentation that would allow viewers to appreciate the project from all angles.
