- LINEAS -

Digital Interactive Canvas - 2022
Created with Processing

In this interactive artwork, the intersecting lines are meticulously arranged to create a moir√© effect, evoking a sense of dynamic movement and optical illusion. The deliberate juxtaposition of strokes against a subdued background serves to intensify the visual impact, drawing the viewer into a dynamic fusion of patterns and shapes. What sets this work apart is its interactivity; viewers can control the movement of lines and visual effects with the mouse, allowing for a personalized exploration of the nuances of the work.
Through the interplay of color, line thickness and spacing, the work invites contemplation of perception and perspective, prompting reflection on the complexities of visual interpretation. By integrating elements of randomness and interactivity, the work offers a fluidity that reflects the changing nature of human perception.
Created in Processing (object-oriented programming language based on Java, for designers and visual artists).
