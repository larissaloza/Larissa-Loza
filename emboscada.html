- EMBOSCADA -

Filming Production Design - 2019

I did the production design for the film Emboscada, directed by Ren√© Herrera and filmed in Mexico City.
It is about Nadine, a building manager, who accompanies a mobster who shows up to search, apartment by apartment, for a corpse that was stolen and that contains something of great value. One after another, the building's inhabitants die.
The entire story takes place in the same building, so my job consisted of taking charge of the visual aesthetics and the assembly of the decoration of each of the sets (complete apartments within the building, the lobby and the roof) in addition to the production of props for the characters.
This film was selected at the 37th Chicago Latino Film Festival and at the Femme Revolution Fest.
