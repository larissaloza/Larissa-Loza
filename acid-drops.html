- ACID DROPS -

Interactive Video Art - 2023
Created with TouchDesigner

This is another interactive work in which I mix art, technology and the movement of the human body.
A motion sensor reads the horizontal, vertical and depth coordinates of the participants' hands, and also registers when the hands are open or clenched. This three-dimensional information is translated into two-dimensional information on a digital canvas.
The movement of the right hand becomes an emitter with which the entire canvas can be painted with a "brush", while the left hand closed in a fist, indicates that you want to restart the emitter or "brush" from zero, having again the whole background in blue color.
This real-time interaction gives the work a sense of immediacy and intimacy, as participants interact directly with the digital medium, shaping and reshaping the visual landscape with their touch.
