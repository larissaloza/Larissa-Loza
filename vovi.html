- VOVI -

3D Art - 2023
Created with Blender

In this ongoing project, I capture the image of my beloved companion, Vovi, a Dachshund and Beagle mix.
The creative process began with a series of photographs in which Vovi appeared from various angles in a neutral pose, which served as a reference for the modeling. Starting with basic geometric shapes, I refined and sculpted the forms, molding them to reflect the organic shapes.
Then followed the process of UV mapping and texture painting, adding depth and realism to the model. And finally, the fur was added.
The next phase will consist of rigging and animation, bringing Vovi's digital representation to life. Maybe later I'll be able to program a basic videogame using Unreal Engine 5.
