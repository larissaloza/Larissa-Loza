VR Experience - 2021
Coded with HTML/CSS/A-frame

In this virtual exploration project programmed in A-frame (web framework based on top of HTML for building virtual reality experiences), I enter the realms of the aquatic, terrestrial and aerial worlds. This interactive virtual space offers users the opportunity to navigate seamlessly between these different environments, either through traditional web navigation with keyboard arrows and mouse controls or through the immersive experience of virtual reality headsets.
Through design and programming, I have created an experimental space in which users can interact and explore the intricacies of these three diverse worlds. From the ocean to the vast expanses of land landscapes and infinite skies.
This project is a testament to the power of technology to push boundaries and immerse users in worlds both familiar and fantastic. Through this virtual journey, I hope to inspire curiosity, wonder and a greater appreciation for the beauty and diversity of our natural world.
You can visit AxQyUzA here. (Due to the heaviness of the web content, it is recommended to access the site from a computer for optimal performance).
